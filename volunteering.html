Volunteering
AREA DIRECTOR

Role Description:

  • Collect and post about potential problems in your district.
  • Aid people who wish to submit problems in their Area.
  • Ensure distribution of CSI magazine in their district

A district may have more than one Area Directors.
On completion of term or during exit, person will awarded with a certificate for their service at CSI.

Write an email to chennaisocietyofinventors@gmail.com saying how you would be able to perform the above roles.
Also attach a scanned copy of any government ID with email.
On getting selected, if the Area Director fails to show sufficient activity for a period of 4 months. He/She is liable to be replaced.
Note:
People who perform extremely well will be offered a chance to join the Innovation Team of CSI.



COLLEGE/SCHOOL REP

Role Description:

  • Collect potential Projects and Ideas which can be featured on the website.
  • Connect students of their respective college/school to the live problems on website and aid in creating valuable solutions.
  • Ensure distribution of CSI magazine amongst students of their college/school.
    • On completion of term or during exit, student will awarded with a certificate for their service at CSI.

Write an email to chennaisocietyofinventors@gmail.com saying how you would be able to perform the above roles.
Also attach a scanned copy of college/school ID with email.
On getting selected, if the Rep fails to show sufficient activity for a period of 4 months. He/She is liable to be replaced.
Note:
Students who perform extremely well will be offered a chance to join the Innovation Team of CSI.



MAGAZINE EDITOR

Role Description:

  • Write articles for the CSI Magazine.
  • Suggest stories that can be covered.
  • Proofread articles that have been received.
    • On completion of term or during exit, person will awarded with a certificate for their service at CSI.

Write an email to chennaisocietyofinventors@gmail.com saying how you would be able to perform the above roles.
Also include your previous writing work,
And attach a scanned copy of your government ID with email.
Note:
Students who perform extremely well will be offered a chance to join the Innovation Team of CSI.



OTHERS

If you aren’t suitable for any of these above roles, but still want to contribute to CSI.
Write an email to chennaisocietyofinventors@gmail.com saying how you would be able to do so.
We’ll try to find you a spot.
