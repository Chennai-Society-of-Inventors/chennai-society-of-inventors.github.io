AREA DIRECTOR

Role Description:

  • Collect and post about potential problems in your district.
  • Aid people who wish to submit problems in their Area.
  • Ensure distribution of CSI magazine in their district

A district may have more than one Area Directors. On completion of term or during exit, person will awarded with a certificate for their service at CSI. Application Process: Write an email to chennaisocietyofinventors@gmail.com saying how you would be able to perform the above roles. Also attach a scanned copy of any government ID with email. On getting selected, if the Area Director fails to show sufficient activity for a period of 4 months. He/She is liable to be replaced. Note: People who perform extremely well will be offered a chance to join the Innovation Team of CSI.
